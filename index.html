About TWD

Founded in 2015, TWD emerged from a passion for creating exceptional digital experiences. What started as a small team of dedicated developers has grown into Melbourne's leading web design studio, serving over 500 successful businesses across Australia.

Our mission is to empower businesses with high-performance websites that drive real results. We believe in combining cutting-edge technology with beautiful design, backed by data-driven optimization and unwavering support for our clients' success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, premium hosting, SSL certificate, regular backups, and 12 months of technical support.

How long does the design process take?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, following our proven 5-step process: Discovery, Design, Development, Testing, and Launch. Complex e-commerce sites may take 8-10 weeks.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and updates?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans including security updates, content changes, performance optimization, and 24/7 monitoring. We also provide training for clients who wish to manage content themselves.

What's your approach to SEO?

Every website is built with SEO best practices including fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, schema markup, and content structure. We also offer advanced SEO packages for ongoing optimization.

What's your payment process?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We offer flexible payment plans for larger projects and accept all major payment methods.
